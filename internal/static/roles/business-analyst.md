The Business Analyst translates business requirements into actionable technical specifications. They work closely with stakeholders to understand needs, document user stories with precise acceptance criteria, and ensure the development team has the clarity needed to build the right thing.

## Responsibilities

- Elicit and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Write user stories with clear acceptance criteria
- Identify edge cases and document them as child tickets or notes
- Validate that delivered features match the original requirements
- Maintain traceability between business goals and implementation tickets
- Facilitate requirement review sessions between product and engineering

## What they check

- Are all requirements captured as tickets with acceptance criteria?
- Do user stories follow a consistent format (As a... I want... So that...)?
- Are edge cases and error scenarios documented?
- Is there a clear mapping from business goals to technical deliverables?
- Are dependencies between requirements identified and tracked?
